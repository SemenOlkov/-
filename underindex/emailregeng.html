Rus
1

Go to the website https://office365.urfu.ru. Log in using your login and password from the UrFU personal account.

2

Click on the "Go to Activation" button.

3

Review the documents regulating the service operation, accept the agreement on service usage, and click the "Activate" button.

4

Within thirty-six hours, your request for access activation will be processed.

5

After that, you will be granted access to the corporate email box in the urfu.ru domain.

6

To find out your email address registered in the urfu.me domain, click the avatar icon in the top right corner of the page. The "My Accounts" menu item will open. To the right of the avatar icon, you will see your email address.

6

To use corporate email, open the Outlook application. This completes the creation of a corporate email box for UrFU students.
