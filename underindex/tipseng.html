A few tips for successful course completion from Linky:

✓ Before you start studying the materials, I recommend turning off notifications, closing unnecessary tabs, and focusing on the course.

✓ When studying lectures, it is advised to take notes, as this will help you remember the information better and prepare for tests.

✓ Do your assignments regularly, such as every week.

✓ Be careful, some tasks have a limited number of attempts, so think carefully about your answers.

✓ Set reminders for upcoming deadlines so you don't miss check dates and lose points.

✓ And finally, don't put tasks off until the last day.
